What is HealthNet?

There is a barrier between doctors and their patients when it comes to scheduling appointments, managing prescriptions, and logging test results. Our goal with HealthNet is to try and bridge that gap. HealthNet is intended to ease the communication between doctors and their patients, and to insure that if a patient needs to transfer from one hospital to another, that all of their information is transferred along with them.
